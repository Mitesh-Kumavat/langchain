cricket is a sport
it is played with a bat and ball
it is played between two teams of eleven players each
this can be played on a field with a 22-yard long pitch in the center
the objective of the game is to score runs by hitting the ball and running between the wickets
the team that scores the most runs wins the match

Famous Cricket Players Of India:
1. Sachin Tendulkar : He is considered one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. He holds the record for the most runs in both One Day Internationals (ODIs) and Test matches.
2. Kapil Dev : He was the captain of the Indian cricket team that won the 1983 Cricket World Cup. He is also one of the greatest all-rounders in cricket history.
3. Virat Kohli : He is one of the best modern-day batsmen and has numerous records to his name, including the fastest century in ODIs by an Indian.
4. MS Dhoni : He is known for his calm demeanor and exceptional leadership skills. He led India to victory in the 2007 T20 World Cup and the 2011 ODI World Cup.
5. Sunil Gavaskar : He was the first player to score 10,000 runs in Test cricket and is regarded as one of the greatest opening batsmen in the history of the game.
6. Rohit Sharma : He is known for his explosive batting and has multiple double centuries in ODIs. He is also the captain of the Indian cricket team.
